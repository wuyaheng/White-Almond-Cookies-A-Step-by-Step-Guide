White Almond Cookies: A Step-by-Step Guide

by Phoebe Yaheng Wu

If you love the nutty taste and crunchy texture of almonds, then you just have to try making these white almond cookies! They're so easy to make, and with each batch, you'll end up with a ton of these yummy treats. Whether you're looking for a tasty breakfast option or a satisfying snack to munch on during a break, these cookies have got you covered. Ingredients
  • 100g Roasted Almond Flakes (toast almond flakes at 180C/350F for 3 minutes)
  • 140g All Purpose Flour
  • 100g Corn Starch
  • 70g Powdered Sugar
  • 60g Almond Flour
  • 2g Salt
  • 130g Peanut Oil
Directions
Step 1:

Mix 120g softened butter until smooth and creamy using an electric mixer on low speed.

Step 2:

Add 80g powdered sugar through a sieve and then mix it with the butter.

Step 3:

Add 2 egg yolks, one at a time, to the mixture until they are fully mixed in.

Step 4:

Add 4g of vanilla extract to the mixture and mix until well combined with the other ingredients.

Step 5:

Add 160g cake flour, 40g almond flour and 2g salt through a sieve to the mixture.

Step 6:

Gently fold all of the ingredients until a dough forms.

Step 7:

Mix 20g cocoa powder and 60g roasted almonds into the dough evenly until they are fully incorporated.

Step 8:

Wrap the dough in parchment paper to form a 1.5-inch cylinder and place the wrapped dough in the refrigerator's freezing compartment for 1 hour.

Step 9:

After 1 hour, unwrap the dough and slice it into 0.5cm thickness cookies.

Step 10:

Bake cookies at 170C/338F for 15 mins on top/bottom heat mode.

The cookies are all done! Time to dig into these yummy homemade chocolate almond cookies!
